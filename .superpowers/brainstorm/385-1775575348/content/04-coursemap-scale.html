Binary Search
Divide the search space in half at each step. O(log n) complexity.

Pros

  • Duolingo-style progress path — proven pattern
  • Entire course visible in one row (no scroll for 12 steps)
  • Hover/click reveals detail — progressive disclosure
  • Very compact on mobile
  • Strong visual distinction: green chain → blue active → grey upcoming

Cons

  • No titles visible until hover (numbers only)
  • At 15+ steps, circles may wrap to 2 rows
  • Harder to scan by topic name
