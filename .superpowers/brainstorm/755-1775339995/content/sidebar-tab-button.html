How it works

Unlocked (hidden)

Tab sits on the left edge of the content area. Blue chevron points right (→ "open sidebar"). Hover near it or click to slide sidebar out as overlay. Sidebar auto-hides when mouse leaves.

Locked (pinned)

Tab sits on the right edge of the sidebar. Chevron points left (← "close sidebar"). Click to unlock — sidebar slides away, content expands. Tab moves to left edge.

Auto-peek

On course completion, sidebar slides in briefly showing the updated progress ring, then slides back. The tab stays in place — just the sidebar animates behind it.
