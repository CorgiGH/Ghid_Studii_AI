**Summary:**
"Proiectarea algoritmilor" is a comprehensive Romanian textbook covering the fundamental principles and advanced topics in the design and analysis of algorithms. The book is structured to guide readers from basic concepts of algorithm definition and analysis to various algorithmic paradigms and specialized topics. It covers essential areas such as sorting, data structures, dynamic programming, greedy algorithms, divide and conquer, graph algorithms, tree structures, geometric algorithms, probabilistic algorithms, algorithms for large number arithmetic, text searching algorithms, and computational complexity theory including P and NP classes. Each chapter typically includes an introduction to the topic, detailed explanations of relevant algorithms, and exercises for practice.
